Boxwise Mobile App Guide

Welcome to the Boxwise Mobile App Guide! This guide will help you get the most out of the mobile features of Boxwise, allowing you to manage your inventory on the go.

Open Boxwise Mobile App

Getting Started

The Boxwise mobile app is a progressive web app (PWA), which means you can access it directly from your mobile browser without needing to install anything from an app store. Here's how to get started:

  1. Open your mobile browser (Safari on iOS, Chrome on Android, etc.)
  2. Navigate to your Boxwise URL (the same URL you use on desktop)
  3. Log in to your account using your existing credentials
  4. Add to home screen (optional) - For easier access, you can add the app to your home screen:
    • On iOS: Tap the share icon, then "Add to Home Screen"
    • On Android: Tap the menu icon, then "Add to Home Screen"

Use Cases

Adding Items on the Go

With the mobile app, you can add items to your inventory right when you purchase them:

  1. Tap the "Add" button in the bottom navigation
  2. Fill in the basic information (name, location, category)
  3. Use the UPC lookup to scan the barcode and automatically fill in product details
  4. Take photos of the item using the "Take Photo" button
  5. Add purchase details (where purchased, price, date)
  6. Tap "Save" to add the item to your inventory

Checking Inventory While Shopping

Avoid duplicate purchases by checking your inventory while shopping:

  1. Tap the shopping cart icon to open the Shopping Assistant
  2. Search for the item by name or scan its barcode
  3. If the item is already in your inventory, you'll see details about it
  4. If it's not in your inventory but was found in the product database, you can add it directly
  5. If it's not found at all, you can still add it manually

Taking Photos of Existing Items

Add photos to existing items in your inventory:

  1. Navigate to the item in your inventory
  2. Tap on the item to view its details
  3. Tap "Edit" to edit the item
  4. Scroll down to the Photos section
  5. Tap "Take Photo" to capture a new photo
  6. Save the item to update it with the new photo

Tips for Mobile Use

Offline Use

The mobile app requires an internet connection to function properly. Make sure you have a stable connection when using the app, especially when adding or editing items.

Battery Usage

Using the camera for barcode scanning and photo capture can drain your battery. Consider closing the app when not in use to conserve battery life.

Storage Space

Photos taken with the app are stored in your account and don't take up space on your device. However, the app itself may cache data locally, so if you're running low on storage, consider clearing your browser cache.

Camera Permissions

The app requires camera permissions for barcode scanning and photo capture. If you accidentally denied these permissions, you'll need to update them in your device settings.
